ABOUT US

At FurniCo Company, our focus is simple. We offer high quality furnishings at affordable prices. Our team of trained buyers source the finest products from all over the world, ensuring we deliver the best quality furniture with modest pricing. With something for every home, we offer a huge range of furniture and homeware products. Each item is carefully selected, keeping our reputation for providing the best products at the best prices in mind. As one of the UK’s leading furniture websites, our customers will always come first. We may also be able to offer discounts for large and multiple orders. We believe that every home deserves the highest quality of furniture. Our options include furnishings for bedrooms, offices, gardens, hallways, kitchens, dining and living areas plus storage facilities. We pride ourselves on delivering the best customer service and we are so confident in our furnishings that we offer a 30 day cancellation policy.*



*Goods must be returned as new in the original packaging. For further information, please see our returns policy.
